Merkel, responding to Trump, says Europe's fate is in its own hands By Reuters   Published: 13:27 BST, 16 January 2017  |  Updated: 13:27 BST, 16 January 2017                e-mail         BERLIN, Jan 16 (Reuters) - Europe's destiny lies in its own hands, German Chancellor Angela Merkel said on Monday in response to comments from U.S. President-elect Donald Trump, who said in a newspaper interview he believed other countries would leave the EU after Britain."I think we Europeans have our fate in our own hands," Merkel told a joint news conference with New Zealand's prime minister.Asked about Trump's criticism of her migrant policy, Merkel said: "I am personally waiting for the inauguration of the U.S. president. Then of course we will work with him on all levels."(Writing by Paul Carrel; Editing by Victoria Bryan)
